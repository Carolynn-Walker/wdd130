Overview

Purpose

Inform potential customers about the companies white water rafting services.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on)
THe target customers are Adults, 18-45, middle class, with an interest in Outdoorsy activities, kayaking, and white water rafting.
What do they want?
They want to explore the outdoors and to white water raft. They want access to kayaks to white water raft with. They want to rent out the gear necessary to white water raft.
How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?
Primarily on desktop/laptops. There will also likely be mobile users.
